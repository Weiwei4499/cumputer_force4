What is Click & Collect?

Click & Collect is an easy way to buy online and pick up your items from your local Computer Force store. Plus it's FREE!

How to use Click & Collect

  • Once you checkout, select the ‘Click & Collect’ tab in the Delivery details section and enter your postcode to see the closest stores that have the item’s you are purchasing in-stock. Please note: Click & Collect is offered subject to availability and depends on the type of product you're purchasing, for example, some special order items and large items cannot be picked up in-store
  • Select your preferred store from the list
  • Pay and complete your order, that’s it! We’ll reserve your items and get them ready for collection during the store opening hours.

    • We’ll contact you as soon as your order is ready for collection After you've placed your order online, wait to hear from us about your collection. We'll send you an email and SMS as soon as your items are ready to collect. Orders are usually ready for collection within an hour of purchase or as soon as the store opens the next day if you purchase outside store hours.

    What to bring when collecting your order

  • Proof of purchase: Including a reference to your order number, your order confirmation email or SMS will have this.
  • Valid physical photo ID: Current driver's licence, passport or Keypass.
  • Proof of age card: If you're collecting items containing MA15+ and R18+ rated content.
